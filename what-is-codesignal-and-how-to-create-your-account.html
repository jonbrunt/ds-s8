What is CodeSignal and How to Create Your Account

CodeSignal is an online skills-based assessment platform for technical hiring and coding skill certification. It's used by many companies to evaluate candidates' coding abilities.

To set up your account, follow these steps:

  1. Use the same email address for both CodeSignal and the Portal to ensure your results sync correctly.

Important: If you use different email addresses for logging into CodeSignal and the Portal, your assessment results will not be tracked in the BloomTech database.
